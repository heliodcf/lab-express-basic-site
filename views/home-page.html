ABOUT WORKS

TOM HOLLAND

Tom Holland is an English actor. He rose to fame in 2008 as a child actor starring in the West End production of Billy Elliot the Musical and in the 2012 film The Impossible. He is best known for portraying the superhero Spider-Man in the Marvel movie franchise, including Captain America: Civil War (2016), Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017) and Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019).
